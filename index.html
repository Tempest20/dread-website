Do you have what it takes to stand before Hell itself?

DREAD is a fanmade map-pack for Doom 2, playable through most modern source-ports. It was heavily inspired by Doom 64 and its unique atmosphere.



It was made using SLADE 3, Ultimate Doom Builder and lots of caffeine.

The map-pack has 10 levels split into two episodes: Teleport Cleanup and Welcome To Hell
